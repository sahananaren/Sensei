## üîò Button Sizes & States

### Primary Button

- **Height**: 48px  
- **Padding**: 16px horizontal  
- **Corner Radius**: 8px  
- **Font Size**: 15px  
- **Color**: Accent color (depends on vision)

### Secondary Button

- **Border**: 1px solid `#A7A7A7`  
- **Background**: Transparent

- **Corner Radius**: 8px  
- **Height**: 44px  

### Disabled State

- **Opacity**: 40%  
- **Background**: Accent or card background  
- **Text Color**: `#A7A7A7`
